The 3 mistakes of my life

by Chetan Bhagat

The book, which was published in 2008, chronicles the narrative of three teenage friends who live in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India: Govind, Ishaan, and Omi. The story follows them as they make their way through adolescence and maturity while looking for their place in the world and working towards their goals.
